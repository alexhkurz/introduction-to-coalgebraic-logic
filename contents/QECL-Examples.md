See the section [The Logic of Chosen Predicate Liftings](QECL-Introduction.md#the-logic-of-a-chosen-set-of-predicate-liftings) for the general setup of many-valued modal logics with truth values in a quantale. The examples below illustrate different choices for the data introduced there.
