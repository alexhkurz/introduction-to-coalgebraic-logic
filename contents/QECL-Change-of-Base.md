# Change of Base

**Definition:** Lax morphism of quantales ...

Every quantale morphism is also a lax morphism of quantales because quantale morphisms preserve identity, order and multiplication.

Every quantale morphism $f:Q\to R$ preserves sups and hence has a right-adjoint $f^\ast:R\to Q$ determined by $f(q)\sqsubseteq r \ \Leftrightarrow \ q\sqsubseteq f^\ast(r)$ or also by $$f^\ast(r)=\bigvee\{q\mid f(q)\sqsubseteq r\}$$
